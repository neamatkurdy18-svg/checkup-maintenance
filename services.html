Preventive Care

Routine Maintenance

Regular AC maintenance prevents costly breakdowns and extends the life of your unit. We recommend servicing every 3–6 months — especially before summer and winter seasons. A well-maintained AC also uses less electricity, saving you money every month.

Our maintenance visit is thorough, fast, and leaves your unit running like new.

What's Included:

  • Filter cleaning & washing
  • Indoor & outdoor coil cleaning
  • Drain pipe flushing
  • Gas pressure check
  • Electrical connections check
  • Full performance test
