Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we receive.

How quickly do you respond?

We typically respond within the hour and aim to be on-site the same day. For emergencies, we respond immediately — any time of day or night.

Do you work on all AC brands?

Yes — we service all major AC brands available in Lebanon, including Daikin, LG, Samsung, Mitsubishi, Carrier, Midea, Gree, Panasonic, and more.

What areas do you cover?

We're based in Beirut and cover all of Greater Beirut and most areas across Lebanon. If you're unsure, just call us — we almost certainly cover your area.

Do you give a quote before starting work?

Always. We inspect the unit, diagnose the problem, and give you a clear, honest quote before any work begins. No surprises on the bill.

Do you offer a warranty on repairs?

Yes — all of our work comes with a workmanship guarantee. If the same issue comes back after our repair, we return and fix it at no extra charge.

What are your payment methods?

We currently accept cash payment on the day of service. Contact us before the visit if you have any questions about payment.

How often should I service my AC?

We recommend servicing every 3–6 months — ideally once before summer and once before winter. Regular maintenance prevents breakdowns and reduces electricity costs.

Do you work on weekends and holidays?

Yes — we're available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including all public holidays. Your comfort doesn't take days off, and neither do we.
